Hogwarts, The Best School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in The World

About

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry was founded by four of the most brilliant witches and wizards of their time. It was founded in the 10th century by Godric Gryffindor, Rowena Ravenclaw, Helga Hufflepuff and Salazar Slytherin. Each of them created a house in their names and thus established one of the best schools in the wizarding world. Many of the individuals in each house have similar traits or qualities as the original founders. Young witches and wizards receive their letters to Hogwarts at the mere age of 11 years old. Before beginning their schooling , each student must first be sorted into their correct houses by the magical Sorting Hat.

Founders

Godric Gryffindor

Godric Amaryllis Gryffindor was a British pure-blood wizard and one of the four founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He was the husband of Salazar Slytherin, father of Laoise Slytherin and Julian Gryffindor, and one of the ancestors of Tom, Kamaria, Andros, Aquarius, and Katara Prince-Slytherin, and ancestor-in-law to Severus and Ginevra Prince-Slytherin.

Rowena Ravenclaw

Rowena Ravenclaw (fl. c. 993) was a Scottish witch and one of the four founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. She was noted for her intelligence and creativity. Helga Hufflepuff

Helga Hufflepuff (fl. c. 993) was a Welsh witch and was one of the four founders of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. While the other founders chose to have special students, she accepted them all without preference, although she did want them to be loyal and hard-working. Helga's portrait remained at Hogwarts by at least the 1980s.

Salazar Slytherin

Salazar Slytherin (fl. c. 993) was a pure-blood wizard, noted for his cunning and determination.
